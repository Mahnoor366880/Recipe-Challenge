Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

Total: Approximately 10 minutes

Preparation: 5 minutes

Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Beat eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil to a non-stick pan.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs into the pan and cook evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Sprinkle optional fillings over half the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully fold and serve hot.
